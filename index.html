About

This page collects lecture notes, course material, problem sets and references in theoretical physics. The aim is to keep useful material publicly available in one place, rather than scattered across private folders, old course pages or email exchanges.

The motivation came from the Anti Gravity Meetings, an informal space initiated by Olivera Miskovic, Nelson Merino and Rodrigo Olea where many of us met to discuss physics outside the usual seminar-room format. This website keeps that practical spirit: make material accessible, readable and easy to find.
